## 3. Privacy & PII Handling âœ…âŒ

### Requirements

- [ ] **Participant IDs used in all notes, findings, and reports**
  - Format: P01, P02, P03, etc.
  - NEVER use real names in shared documents

- [ ] **PII stored separately from research findings**
  - PII (names, emails, phone numbers) kept in secure location
  - Only accessible to research team
  - Not included in findings documents or reports

- [ ] **All shared documents are anonymized**
  - Session notes: Participant IDs only
  - Findings: Participant IDs only
  - Reports: Participant IDs only
  - Quotes: "P01 said..." not "[Name] said..."

- [ ] **Data retention policy followed**
  - Autodesk typically retains research data for 2-3 years
  - After that, data is deleted according to policy
  - Check with Research Ops for specific guidelines

### What is PII (Personally Identifiable Information)?

- Real names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- IP addresses
- Photos/videos showing faces (unless consented for public use)
