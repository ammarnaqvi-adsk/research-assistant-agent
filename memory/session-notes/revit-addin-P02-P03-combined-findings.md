**Frequency**: 2/2 participants (100%)
**Severity**: 游댮 High

**Severity**: 游댮 High

## What Worked Well (2/2 participants)

3. **Visibility toggles** - Both found unchecking boxes intuitive
4. **Element mapping concept** - P02: "Immediately understood" Revit type selection
